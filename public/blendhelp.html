how to blend

  1. Log in using your User ID.
  2. Click pick a genre and select your preferred genre from the list.
  3. Click the "Generate" button and watch as blendify created your playlist.
  4. Enjoy and share the playlist!

Q: Can I use blendify without a Spotify account?
Unfortunately, you need a Spotify account to use Blendify as it pulls data from Spotify to create playlists based on your preferences.

Q: How does Blendify select the songs for my playlist?
blendify uses your chosen genre and your music preferences. It looks at your Spotify listening history, favorite artists, and top tracks to create the playlist.

Q: Is blendify free to use?
Yes, blendify is completely free to use.
