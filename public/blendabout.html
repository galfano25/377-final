about

blendify makes creating playlists easier by allowing you to create personalized playlists tailored to your musical tastes. Whether you're vibing to pop, metal, jazz, etc., blendify ensures that your favorite songs and preferences are represented in the perfect mix.

why blendify?

We love the blend feature on Spotify, but it's missing one important feature: the ability to control the genre and vibe. blendify solves that problem by allowing you to curate a perfect playlist that matches your preferences, without having to put it together yourself.
